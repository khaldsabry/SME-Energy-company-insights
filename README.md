Significant churn problem • Driven by power-liberalization of the energy market in Europe • Problem is largest in the SME segment

Utilized important customer attributes to identify potential churners and help improve
customer retention strategies.on two datasets - price data - clients data.

Just completed a project leveraging Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to deeply analyze the data. These powerful Python libraries helped unveil crucial insights and visualize trends, making complex data more accessible and actionable
